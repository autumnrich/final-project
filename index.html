By Element

Seeing the world in terms of elements is a tradition that goes back thousands of years throughout many cultures. The elements represent the basic principles of life, and different systems have delineated the elements in various ways.

Western Astrology uses four elements...Fire, Earth, Air and Water...and each of the 12 zodiac signs falls into one of these categories.

Air

Air signs are known for being more detached and cerebral than others. Air signs aren’t overly emotional, which gives them the ability to think logically and to achieve a broad overview of their idea.
